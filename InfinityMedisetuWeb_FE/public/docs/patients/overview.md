# Patients — Quick Reference

Central repository of every patient who has interacted with the clinic.

---

## Finding Patients

| Search By | How |
|-----------|-----|
| Phone number | Most reliable identifier |
| Full name | May have spelling variations |
| Email | If registered |
| Patient ID | Unique system identifier |

---

## Registering a New Patient

### Required Fields
- Full name
- Mobile number

### Optional (Recommended)
- Email, gender, date of birth
- Address, alternate mobile
- Emergency contact details
- Insurance information

> Important: Always search before creating. Duplicates split medical history and cause serious problems downstream.

## Patient Profile Contents

| Section | What's Inside |
|---------|---------------|
| **Personal Details** | Contact, demographics, insurance |
| **Medical History** | Past illnesses, surgeries, chronic conditions, family history |
| **Allergies** | With severity levels and reactions |
| **Medications** | Current meds with dosage |
| **Consultations** | Every past visit — click to expand |
| **Prescriptions** | Every prescription ever generated |
| **Lab Reports** | All results with reference ranges |
| **Gallery** | Clinical images from all visits |
| **Billing** | Complete payment history |
| **No-Show Records** | Missed appointment history |

## Timeline View

All interactions displayed chronologically. Each entry shows:
- Consultation date
- Doctor name
- Diagnosis
- Status

Click any entry to expand full vitals, notes, prescriptions, and lab orders.

---

## Tips for Better Records

- Complete profiles at registration — saves time later
- Update allergy info whenever discovered
- Keep emergency contacts current
- Record medical history on first visit
- Verify phone number during registration

## Subscribed Patients

View patients enrolled in clinic subscription plans:
- Plan name and validity period
- Benefits consumed vs remaining
- Payment status and renewal tracking

## Patient Gallery

Aggregates all clinical images uploaded during consultations:
- Wound progress photos, skin conditions, diagnostic images
- Each tagged with description and linked to specific appointment
- Useful for tracking chronic condition progression over time
