# Welcome to MediSetu

MediSetu is a cloud-based clinic management platform that brings together patient records, appointment scheduling, digital prescriptions, laboratory operations, pharmacy inventory, and financial tracking into one integrated system.

This tour guide walks you through the platform's core features by role. Select your role from the sidebar to jump to the section most relevant to you.

---

## Platform Roles

| Role | What You Do |
|------|-------------|
| **Administrator** | Configure clinic, manage staff accounts, oversee operations, handle billing and subscription |
| **Doctor** | Manage appointments, conduct consultations, write prescriptions, order lab tests |
| **Receptionist** | Book appointments, register patients, check-in arrivals, process payments |
| **Lab Assistant** | Receive test orders, collect samples, process tests, enter and deliver results |
| **Pharmacist** | Manage inventory, dispense prescriptions, handle invoicing and suppliers |
| **Patient** | Book appointments via the app, view queue position, access prescriptions and lab reports |

## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Open MediSetu in your browser (Chrome recommended)
2. Enter the email and password provided by your clinic admin
3. On first login, go to **Profile → Security** and change your password
4. Complete your profile at **Profile → Edit** (name, contact, qualifications)

### New Clinic Setup (Admins)

If setting up a new clinic, the platform walks you through a guided wizard:
1. Clinic information (name, address, logo)
2. Subscription plan selection
3. Primary doctor profile
4. Services and pricing
5. Availability schedule

Complete it end-to-end before inviting other staff.

### Navigation

- **Sidebar** — Shows modules available to your role (collapses on mobile)
- **Global Search** — Find patients by name/phone, appointments by date, medicines by name
- **Notifications Bell** — Real-time alerts for appointment updates, lab results, stock alerts
- **Help Icon (?)** — Context-sensitive guidance on any screen

## Key Capabilities

### Real-Time Sync

Every action updates instantly for all connected users. When reception checks a patient in, the doctor sees the queue update immediately. No refreshing needed.

### Role-Based Access

Each user sees only what they need. A receptionist cannot access clinical notes. A doctor cannot manage pharmacy inventory. This keeps things focused and protects patient privacy.

### Continuity of Care

Every consultation, prescription, lab result, and clinical image is permanently linked to the patient's profile. Full history is always one click away.

### Digital Prescriptions

Write prescriptions digitally using the in-app builder with templates, or write by hand and scan with the AI-powered notepad scanner. Either way, the result is saved to the patient's record.

### Smart Notifications

WhatsApp reminders, in-app push notifications, and queue position updates keep patients and staff informed automatically.

## Security Essentials

- Use a strong, unique password (8+ characters with numbers and special characters)
- Always log out when leaving your workstation
- Never share credentials — ask your admin to create separate accounts
- Check **Profile → Security** for active sessions and terminate unrecognized ones

## Quick Troubleshooting

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| Can't log in | Check email/password, try "Forgot Password", confirm account is active |
| Pages loading slowly | Refresh page, clear browser cache, use Chrome |
| Feature appears locked | Check subscription plan at **Profile → Subscription** |
| Real-time updates stopped | Refresh the page to reconnect WebSocket |
| Data not saving | Check internet, fill all required fields (marked with *) |
