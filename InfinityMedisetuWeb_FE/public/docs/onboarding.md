## Overview

The onboarding process is designed to help you set up your clinic quickly and efficiently. Depending on your role, you'll complete between 3-5 steps:

**For Clinic Owners/Administrators:**
1. Your Profile
2. Clinic Details
3. Review & Submit

**For Doctors:**
1. Your Profile
2. Services & Pricing
3. Doctor Availability
4. Review & Submit

> **Note:** All information can be edited later from your profile settings. Don't worry about getting everything perfect on the first try!

**Estimated Time:** 10-15 minutes

## Step 1: Your Profile

Set up your professional profile with essential information.

### Required Information

**1. Personal Details**

- **Full Name** (required)
  - Enter your full name as per your medical registration
  - Example: "Dr. Rajesh Kumar"

- **Mobile Number** (required)
  - 10-digit mobile number for contact
  - This will be your primary contact number
  - Example: "9876543210"

- **Alternate Contact** (optional)
  - Secondary contact number
  - Useful for emergencies or if primary is unavailable

**2. Professional Details**

- **Registration Number** (required)
  - Your medical registration/license number
  - Example: "MH/12345/2020"
  - This will be verified during approval

- **Speciality** (required)
  - Select your medical specialization from dropdown
  - Common options: General Physician, Cardiologist, Dermatologist, Pediatrician, etc.
  - If your speciality isn't listed, select "Other" and specify

### Validation Rules

- Name: Cannot be empty
- Mobile: Must be exactly 10 digits
- Alternate Contact: If provided, must be 10 digits
- Registration Number: Cannot be empty
- Speciality: Must be selected from dropdown

### Tips

- ✅ Use your official name matching your registration certificate
- ✅ Double-check your mobile number - this is how patients will contact you
- ✅ Ensure registration number is accurate for smooth approval

> **Important:** Your registration number will be verified. Providing incorrect information may delay approval.

### Next Step

Click **"Save & Continue"** to proceed to the next step.

---

## Step 2: Clinic Details

**This step is for Clinic Owners/Administrators only.** Doctors can skip to [Step 3: Services & Pricing](#step-3-services--pricing).

Set up your clinic's basic information and location.

### Required Information

**1. Basic Information**

- **Clinic Name** (required)
  - Your clinic's official name
  - Example: "Care Plus Multispeciality Clinic"
  - This will appear in patient searches

- **Clinic Contact Number** (required)
  - 10-digit clinic inquiry contact
  - Main reception or inquiry number
  - Example: "9876543210"

- **Tagline** (optional)
  - Brief description of your clinic
  - Example: "Your Health, Our Priority"
  - Maximum 100 characters

**2. Clinic Logo** (optional)

- Click "Upload Logo" or drag & drop
- **Requirements:**
  - Format: JPG, PNG, JPEG, or WEBP
  - Maximum size: 3MB
  - Recommended: Square image (500x500px or larger)
  - Appears on patient-facing pages

> **Tip:** A professional logo builds trust. If you don't have one yet, you can add it later from profile settings.

**3. Location Details**

- **Search Location** (required)
  - Type your clinic address in the search box
  - Select from Google Maps suggestions
  - Pin will be placed automatically

- **Pin Location on Map** (required)
  - Verify the pin is at the correct location
  - Drag the pin if adjustment is needed
  - Zoom in for precise placement

- **Complete Address** (required)
  - **House/Building/Road/Area:** Detailed street address
  - **City & State:** Select from dropdown
  - **Pincode:** 6-digit postal code
  
  Example:
  ```
  House/Building/Road/Area: Shop No. 5, Ramnagar Society, MG Road
  City: Mumbai
  State: Maharashtra
  Pincode: 400001
  ```

### Validation Rules

- Clinic Name: Cannot be empty
- Mobile: Must be exactly 10 digits
- Logo: Max 3MB, valid image format
- Location: Must be set on map
- Address fields: All required fields must be filled
- Pincode: Must be exactly 6 digits

### Tips

- ✅ Use your clinic's registered name
- ✅ Ensure the map pin is accurate - this helps patients find you
- ✅ Provide detailed address including landmarks
- ✅ Use a high-quality logo for professional appearance

> **Note:** Accurate location is crucial for patient discovery. Double-check the map pin before proceeding.

### Next Step

Click **"Save & Continue"** to proceed to review and submit.

## Step 3: Services & Pricing

**This step is for Doctors only.** Set up the services you provide and their pricing.

### Adding Services

For each service you provide, enter:

**1. Service Name** (required)
- Name of the service/consultation type
- Examples:
  - "General Consultation"
  - "Follow-up Consultation"
  - "Cardiology Consultation"
  - "Skin Treatment"

**2. Price** (required)
- Consultation fee in INR (₹)
- Enter numbers only
- Example: "500" for ₹500

**3. Duration** (required)
- Service duration in days
- For regular consultations, typically 1 day
- For treatment plans, can be longer (e.g., 7, 15, 30 days)

**4. Additional Services** (optional)
- Any additional services included
- Example: "Includes ECG and basic blood pressure check"
- Helps patients understand what's included

### Adding Multiple Services

1. Click **"+ Add Service"** to add another service
2. Fill in the service details
3. Repeat for all services you provide
4. You can add as many services as needed

### Managing Services

- **Edit:** Click the edit icon to modify a service
- **Delete:** Click the delete icon to remove a service
- **Reorder:** Drag services to change their order (if supported)

### Validation Rules

- At least 1 service must be added
- Service name: Cannot be empty
- Price: Must be a positive number
- Duration: Must be a positive number

### Service Pricing Tips

- ✅ Set competitive prices for your area
- ✅ Offer both regular and follow-up consultation options
- ✅ Be transparent about what's included
- ✅ Consider package deals for regular patients

### Common Service Examples

**General Physician:**
- General Consultation - ₹500 - 1 day
- Follow-up Visit - ₹300 - 1 day
- Health Checkup - ₹1000 - 1 day

**Dermatologist:**
- Skin Consultation - ₹800 - 1 day
- Acne Treatment - ₹1500 - 7 days
- Hair Treatment - ₹2000 - 15 days

**Cardiologist:**
- Cardiology Consultation - ₹1200 - 1 day
- ECG & Consultation - ₹1500 - 1 day
- Follow-up - ₹800 - 1 day

> **Tip:** You can always add, edit, or remove services later from your profile settings.

### Next Step

Click **"Save & Continue"** to proceed to availability setup.

## Step 4: Doctor Availability

**This step is for Doctors only.** Set up your working hours and availability.

### Setting Working Days

**1. Select Working Days**

Check the days you're available:
- ☐ Monday
- ☐ Tuesday
- ☐ Wednesday
- ☐ Thursday
- ☐ Friday
- ☐ Saturday
- ☐ Sunday

> **Note:** You must select at least one working day.

### Setting Time Slots

For each selected day:

**1. Start Time** (required)
- When you start seeing patients
- Example: "09:00 AM"
- Use 12-hour format

**2. End Time** (required)
- When you finish seeing patients
- Example: "05:00 PM"
- Must be after start time

**3. Slot Duration** (required)
- How long each appointment lasts (in minutes)
- Common options: 15, 20, 30, 45, 60 minutes
- Example: "30" for 30-minute appointments

**4. Step Minutes** (optional)
- Buffer time between appointments (in minutes)
- Useful for documentation, preparation
- Example: "5" for 5-minute buffer
- Leave blank if no buffer needed

### Adding Breaks

You can add breaks during your working hours:

**1. Break Type**
- Lunch Break
- Tea Break
- Personal Break
- Meeting

**2. Break Times**
- Start Time: When break begins
- End Time: When break ends
- Must be within working hours

**3. Managing Breaks**
- Click **"+ Add Break"** to add a break
- Click delete icon to remove a break
- You can add multiple breaks per day

### Break Examples

```
Morning Shift: 09:00 AM - 01:00 PM
Lunch Break: 01:00 PM - 02:00 PM
Evening Shift: 02:00 PM - 06:00 PM
Tea Break: 04:00 PM - 04:15 PM
```

### Special Date Availability (Optional)

Set custom availability for specific dates:

**1. Select Date**
- Click calendar icon
- Choose future date
- Useful for holidays, special clinics

**2. Availability Status**
- **Available:** You'll be working on this date
- **Unavailable:** You won't be available

**3. Custom Time Slots** (if available)
- Set different times than your regular schedule
- Useful for special clinics or half-days

### Validation Rules

- At least 1 working day must be selected
- End time must be after start time
- Slot duration must be positive
- Breaks must be within working hours
- Break end time must be after start time

### Availability Tips

- ✅ Start with realistic hours you can commit to
- ✅ Include buffer time between appointments
- ✅ Schedule breaks for lunch and rest
- ✅ Consider peak hours in your area
- ✅ You can always update availability later

### Common Availability Patterns

**Full Day Clinic:**
```
Monday - Saturday: 09:00 AM - 06:00 PM
Lunch Break: 01:00 PM - 02:00 PM
Slot Duration: 30 minutes
```

**Morning Clinic:**
```
Monday - Saturday: 09:00 AM - 01:00 PM
No breaks
Slot Duration: 20 minutes
```

**Split Shift:**
```
Monday - Friday
Morning: 09:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Evening: 05:00 PM - 08:00 PM
Slot Duration: 30 minutes
```

> **Important:** Patients can only book appointments during your available slots. Ensure your schedule is accurate.

### Next Step

Click **"Save & Continue"** to proceed to review and submit.

## Step 5: Review & Submit

Final step before submitting your profile for verification.

### What to Review

**1. Your Information**
- Review all entered details
- Check for any errors or typos
- Ensure phone numbers are correct

**2. Clinic/Service Information**
- Verify clinic details (if applicable)
- Check service prices (if applicable)
- Confirm availability schedule (if applicable)

### Making Changes

- Click **"Edit"** next to any section to make changes
- You'll be taken back to that step
- After editing, return to review page

### Subscription Selection

Choose a subscription plan:

**Free Plan (Trial)**
- Limited features
- Good for testing the platform
- Can upgrade anytime

**Premium Plans**
- Full feature access
- More appointment slots
- Priority support
- Various pricing tiers

> **Special Offer:** First-time users may be eligible for 1 month free on any plan!

### Submitting for Verification

**1. Review Terms**
- Read the terms and conditions
- Check the confirmation box
- Ensure you agree to platform policies

**2. Submit**
- Click **"Submit for Verification"**
- Your profile will be sent for admin review
- You'll see a confirmation message

### What Happens After Submission?

1. **Verification Screen Appears**

   - Displays estimated review time
   - Provides contact support options

2. **Admin Reviews Your Profile**
   - Verifies your registration number
   - Checks clinic details
   - Ensures all information is complete

3. **Approval Notification**
   - You'll receive notification when approved
   - Status updates via email/SMS
   - Can check status anytime

### Submission Checklist

Before submitting, ensure:

- ☐ All required fields are filled
- ☐ Mobile numbers are correct
- ☐ Registration number is accurate
- ☐ Clinic location is precise (if applicable)
- ☐ Services are properly priced (if applicable)
- ☐ Availability schedule is set (if applicable)
- ☐ Profile picture/logo uploaded (optional)
- ☐ You've reviewed all information
- ☐ Terms and conditions accepted

> **Tip:** Take a screenshot of your submitted information for your records.

## Verification Process

### Timeline

**Typical Review Time:** 24-48 hours

Factors affecting review time:
- Time of submission
- Completeness of information
- Verification of registration number
- Holiday/weekend submissions

### Verification Steps

**1. Document Verification**
- Medical registration number checked
- Credentials validated
- License status confirmed

**2. Information Review**
- Profile details checked for completeness
- Clinic information verified (if applicable)
- Contact details validated

**3. Final Approval**
- Admin approves profile
- Account activated
- Welcome email sent

### Checking Status

**From Verification Screen:**
1. Click **"Re-check Status"** button
2. System will fetch latest status
3. Page updates automatically

**Status Types:**

- **⏳ Pending:** Under review by admin
- **✅ Approved:** Profile verified and activated
- **❌ Rejected:** Needs corrections (see notes)

### If Rejected

Don't worry! Rejection is usually due to minor issues:

**Common Rejection Reasons:**
- Invalid registration number
- Incomplete information
- Unclear clinic location
- Pricing inconsistencies
- Photo/logo quality issues

**What to Do:**
1. Read the rejection notes carefully
2. Click **"Update Profile"** button
3. Fix the mentioned issues
4. Resubmit for verification

> **Note:** You can resubmit as many times as needed. Take your time to get everything right.

### Need Help?

If verification is taking longer than expected:

1. **Check Spam Folder:** Emails might be filtered
2. **Verify Mobile Number:** SMS notifications require correct number
3. **Contact Support:** Use the help button for assistance
4. **Check Status Regularly:** Use "Re-check Status" button

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue: Can't proceed to next step**

**Solutions:**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check for validation error messages
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache and retry

**Issue: Mobile number not accepted**

**Solutions:**
- Ensure it's exactly 10 digits
- Remove any spaces or dashes
- Don't include country code (+91)
- Use numbers only, no letters

**Issue: File upload fails**

**Solutions:**
- Check file size (max 3MB)
- Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, JPEG, WEBP)
- Try compressing the image
- Use a different browser
- Check internet connection

**Issue: Location not found on map**

**Solutions:**
- Type full address including city and state
- Use Google Maps format
- Try searching nearby landmark
- Manually drag the pin to correct location
- Ensure location services are enabled

**Issue: Can't add services**

**Solutions:**
- Fill all required fields (name, price, duration)
- Enter positive numbers only
- Check for JavaScript errors in console
- Try refreshing the page
- Use Chrome or Firefox browser

**Issue: Availability times not saving**

**Solutions:**
- Ensure end time is after start time
- Check slot duration is positive
- Verify breaks are within working hours
- Select at least one working day
- Try saving each day individually

**Issue: Verification taking too long**

**Solutions:**
- Normal time: 24-48 hours
- Check spam folder for emails
- Use "Re-check Status" button
- Contact support after 48 hours
- Verify registration number is correct

### Browser Issues

**Recommended Browsers:**
- ✅ Google Chrome (latest version)
- ✅ Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
- ✅ Safari (latest version)
- ✅ Microsoft Edge (latest version)

**Browser Settings:**
- Enable JavaScript
- Allow cookies
- Disable ad blockers (temporarily)
- Clear cache if experiencing issues

### Mobile Device Issues

**For Best Experience:**
- Use stable WiFi connection
- Rotate to landscape for forms
- Ensure sufficient storage space
- Update browser to latest version
- Try desktop version if issues persist

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How long does onboarding take?**

A: Typically 10-15 minutes to complete the form, plus 24-48 hours for verification.

---

**Q: Can I save and continue later?**

A: Yes! Your progress is automatically saved after each step. You can return anytime using the same login.

---

**Q: Can I edit information after submission?**

A: Yes, but only after approval. Once approved, you can edit most information from your profile settings. Some fields (like registration number) may require admin approval for changes.

---

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**

A: You can go back and edit any step before submission. After submission, if rejected, you can update and resubmit.

---

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow HIPAA-compliant security standards. Your data is stored securely and never shared without permission.

---

### Profile Questions

**Q: Can I add multiple specializations?**

A: Currently, you can select one primary specialization. Contact support for multiple specialization needs.

---

**Q: Can I change my mobile number later?**

A: Yes, from profile settings. However, it requires OTP verification on both old and new numbers.

---

**Q: Do I need a medical registration?**

A: Yes, a valid medical registration number is mandatory for doctors. This is verified during approval.

---

### Clinic Questions

**Q: Can I add multiple clinics?**

A: Yes! Premium plans support multiple clinic locations. You can add more clinics from profile settings after initial setup.

---

**Q: What if I don't have a clinic logo?**

A: Logo is optional. You can add it later. However, a professional logo helps build patient trust.

---

**Q: Can I change clinic address later?**

A: Yes, from clinic settings. However, frequent changes may require re-verification.

---

### Services & Pricing Questions

**Q: Can I offer free consultations?**

A: Yes, set the price to ₹0 for free consultations. This is useful for follow-ups or special camps.

---

**Q: How do I add more services later?**

A: Go to Profile → Services & Pricing → Add New Service. You can add, edit, or remove services anytime.

---

**Q: Can I have different prices for different days?**

A: Currently, service prices are fixed. For special pricing, create a separate service (e.g., "Weekend Consultation").

---

### Availability Questions

**Q: Can I have different timings for different days?**

A: Yes! Each day can have its own start time, end time, and breaks.

---

**Q: Can I block specific dates?**

A: Yes, use the Special Date Availability feature to mark holidays or unavailable dates.

---

**Q: What if I want emergency appointments?**

A: You can enable walk-in appointments or reduce slot duration for more flexibility. Contact support for emergency booking features.

**Q: Can I change availability daily?**

A: Yes, from your dashboard. However, existing booked appointments remain unless you cancel them.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: What happens if I don't choose a plan?**

### Verification Questions
