# Subscription & Billing — Quick Reference

Your clinic operates under a subscription plan that defines features and resource limits.

---

## Plans

| Plan | Best For | Key Features |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Free** | Getting started | Basic dashboard, limited accounts, core features |
| **Pro** | Growing clinics | Full analytics, pharmacy, lab, smart prescriptions, WhatsApp |
| **Custom** | Enterprise | Tailored limits, priority support |

---

## Resource Limits

| Resource | What Happens at Limit |
|----------|----------------------|
| Doctor accounts | Cannot add more doctors |
| Staff accounts | Cannot add more staff |
| WhatsApp messages | Monthly quota exhausted — notifications stop |
| Storage duration | Older records may become inaccessible |
| Lab integration | Feature unavailable on Free |
| Smart prescriptions | AI features unavailable on Free |

> Your total limit = base plan limit + add-on purchases.

---

---

## How to Purchase

1. Go to **Subscription → Add-Ons** or **View Plans**
2. Select what you need
3. Choose billing cycle (monthly/yearly)
4. Pay via Razorpay (UPI, Card, Net Banking)
5. Active immediately

---

## Billing History

View all past transactions, amounts, dates. Download receipts for individual payments.

---

## What Happens on Expiry

- Downgraded to Free plan limits
- Data is NOT deleted
- Premium features become restricted
- Resubscribe anytime to restore access

---

## Tips

- Choose yearly to save ~20%
- Check limits before approaching caps (Profile → Subscription)
- Set a monthly reminder to review usage vs limits
- Locked features show a lock icon — upgrade from the same page
