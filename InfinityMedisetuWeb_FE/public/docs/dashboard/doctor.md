# Doctor Dashboard

Your personal practice overview — today's schedule, patient queue, and performance at a glance.

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Total Earnings** | Revenue from your consultations with growth % |
| **Total Appointments** | Your cumulative bookings with period comparison |
| **Confirmed Appointments** | Visits confirmed, awaiting patient arrival |
| **Pending Appointments** | Not yet confirmed — may need follow-up |

---

## Appointment Statistics Chart

Monthly comparison of your completed vs cancelled appointments.

**What to watch for:**
- Rising cancellations → check if scheduling is too tight
- Consistent completions → healthy practice rhythm
- Seasonal dips → normal during holidays, plan accordingly

---

## Patient Queue (Live)

Shows patients who have been **checked in by reception** and are physically waiting.

- Updates the moment reception marks an arrival
- Helps you plan consultation order
- A growing queue means you're running behind

---

## Pending Queue

Patients scheduled today but **not yet checked in**. They may:
- Be on their way
- Have forgotten their appointment
- Need a reminder call from reception

---

## Recent Patients

Quick-access list of your most recent consultations. Useful for:
- Fast follow-up without searching
- Checking if a lab result came back for yesterday's patient
- Reviewing notes from earlier today

---

## Call Reception

In the header bar, use the **Call Reception** button:

| Call Type | When to Use |
|-----------|-------------|
| **General Call** | Need any front-desk assistance (file, message, question) |
| **Next Patient** | Ready to see whoever is next in the queue |

Reception receives the alert instantly and responds.

---

## Best Practices

- **Start of day:** Review full schedule before seeing any patients
- **Between patients:** Glance at the queue to know who's next
- **End of day:** Check for pending lab results that need follow-up
- **Before logging off:** Set availability for next working day
