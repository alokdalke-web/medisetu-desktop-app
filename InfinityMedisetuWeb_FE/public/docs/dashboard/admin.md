# Admin Dashboard

Your command center for clinic-wide operations. Everything you need to assess performance at a glance.

---

## Key Metrics (Top Cards)

| Metric | What It Shows | Healthy Sign |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Total Earnings** | Revenue with % vs previous period | Steady or growing |
| **Total Appointments** | Booking count with trend | Consistent week-over-week |
| **No-Show Count** | Patients who missed appointments | Below 10% of total |
| **Active Patients** | Patients with recent activity | Growing month-over-month |

---

## Revenue Overview Chart

Time-series chart with daily/weekly revenue and appointment count overlay.

**What to watch for:**
- Revenue dropping while appointments are steady → average revenue per visit is falling
- Sudden spikes or dips → investigate specific days for anomalies
- Weekday vs weekend patterns → adjust scheduling accordingly

---

## Appointment Status Breakdown

Side-by-side comparison: Completed, Confirmed, Pending, Cancelled, No-Show.

| Status | Healthy Range |
|--------|--------------|
| Completed | 70-85% of total |
| No-Show | Below 10% |
| Cancelled | Below 15% |

---

## Patient Growth Overview

Tracks new registrations over time.

- **Flat line** → acquisition problem, review marketing
- **Growing** → healthy clinic growth
- **Declining** → investigate patient churn

---

## No-Show Trend Chart

Spot seasonal patterns or sudden spikes.

- **Wednesday spike?** → check if a specific doctor has scheduling issues on that day
- **Month-end increase?** → patients may forget appointments during busy periods

---

## Symptom Statistics

Top reported symptoms across all consultations. Useful for:
- Identifying local outbreaks (sudden cluster of fever cases)
- Seasonal trends (allergies in spring, flu in winter)
- Service planning (high demand for specific consultations)

---

## Live Panels

### Patient Queue
Everyone currently checked in and waiting. Updates in real-time.
- **Growing queue** → a doctor is running behind, intervene early
- **Empty queue with pending appointments** → patients may not be checking in

### Recent Patients
Quick-access list of latest interactions for fast follow-up.
