# Receptionist Dashboard

Built for front-desk situational awareness — what's happening right now and what needs your attention.

---

## Key Panels

| Panel | What It Shows | Why It Matters |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| **Today's Appointments** | Live count of scheduled visits | Know how busy the day is |
| **Pending Check-ins** | Patients with appointments, not yet arrived | Focus when patients walk in |
| **Walk-in Queue** | Patients without appointments waiting | Manage expectations |
| **Upcoming Slots** | Next available slots across all doctors | Answer "when is earliest?" instantly |
| **Recent Bookings** | Last few appointments created/modified | Verify or undo recent actions |
| **Doctor Availability** | Who's working and their next open slot | Direct patients to right doctor |

---

## Quick Actions

One-click shortcuts at the top of your dashboard:

- **Create New Appointment** — Skip menu navigation
- **Register Walk-in** — Direct to registration form

---

## Real-Time Updates

The dashboard updates automatically:
- When a doctor starts/completes a consultation
- When another staff member books an appointment
- When a patient cancels or reschedules

No need to refresh the page.

---

## Start of Shift Routine

1. Open dashboard — review today's full schedule
2. Check which doctors are working (Doctor Availability panel)
3. Cross-check bookings vs doctor availability
4. If any doctor is unavailable → contact their scheduled patients immediately
5. Check yesterday's no-shows → follow up today

---

## During the Day

- Check patients in promptly the moment they arrive
- Use **Upcoming Slots** to answer phone inquiries quickly
- Watch for doctors signaling "Next Patient" via the call system
- Monitor walk-in queue and communicate wait times

---

## End of Shift

- Review any appointments still showing as "Scheduled" where the patient never arrived
- Log these as No-Show (don't leave overnight)
- Verify payment records for the day match what was collected
